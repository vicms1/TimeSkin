## Overview
*TimeSkin* is a computational and conceptual framework designed to model adaptive physical laws based on local space-time contexts, inspired by advances in relativity, quantum mechanics, and modern cosmology (2025)

‚Ä¢ It's a "skin" of time, a contextual layer that envelops and modulates how physical laws are perceived or applied at a given moment and place.
‚Ä¢ It's inspired by contemporary physics phenomena:
‚Ä¢ General relativity: the local curvature of space-time.
‚Ä¢ Quantum mechanics: entanglement and nonlocality.
